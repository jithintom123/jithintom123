Things To Do

Vagamon is a perfect place for people who loves nature and a little bit of adventure too.
You can enjoy the spectacular view the place offers and also enjoy variuos activities like trekking,paragliding,rock climbing etc.

1. Trek to Murugan Mala

Murugan Mala is an ideal place for a short trek which will take around 40 minutes.Hiking this hill is tough, but you wont regret it as the cold fresh breeze and the amazing beauty of the nature will make you speechless. Don't miss to click a picture at the Peacock Statue.

2. Vagamon Pine Forest Trek

"The pine forest adds an ounce of beauty more to the heavenly charm of Vagamon. The forest has a large area covered with lofty pine trees and they all grow on steep edges. So the forest is a nice place for a soft trek. Families, couples or a group of friends can even enjoy a picnic in the forest."

3. Trek to Thangal Para

Thangal para is a Muslim pilgrimage centre in Vagamon and an amazing trekking location which is 400 foot high. The trek is worth the pain and the view from the top will take your breath away.

4. Kurisumala Trek

A popular Christian Pilgrimage in Vagamon. It's followers come from various part of the country to climb up the hills with wooden crosses.

5. Boat Ride at Vagamon Lake

Vagamon lake is surrounded by lush green hills and tea estates which adds more beauty while having a ride on this lake. The lake is not a very huge one, but the serene nature surrounded by makes this place popular among tourists.

6. Paragliding

Vagamon lake is surrounded by lush green hills and tea estates which adds more beauty while having a ride on this lake. The lake is not a very huge one, but the serene nature surrounded by makes this place popular among tourists.

7. Uliponni Wildlife Sanctuary

Ulipooni Wildlife Sanctuary is an ideal escape from the hectic urban life. Located 10 km from the city centre, the serene atmosphere makes for a perfect picnic spot. The sanctuary is home to many animals including elephants and tigers. You can also enjoy a boat ride here and have a memorable day out on your trip to Vagamon. It is indeed one of the best tourist places in Vagamon.

7. Trekking to Suicide Point

At a distance of 8 km from Vagamon Bus Stop, Paragliding Point (also known as Suicide Point) is a spectacular viewpoint situated at Amreuthamedu in Vagamon, Kerala. Also known as Paragliding Viewpoint, it is one of the popular vantage points in the hill town, and among the must include places in Vagamon tour.
