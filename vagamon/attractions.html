Vagamon's enticing attractions

Want to explore the scenic beauty of Vagamon? Here is the list of Vagamon's beautiful places that will take you to the mesmerizing beauty of nature.
You can take the aid of your resort guide so that you don't miss a chance to explore these breathtaking views of Vagamon.

1. Kurisumala

Kurisumala Ashram is a Trappist Monastery in Syro-Malankara Catholic Church in the Sahya Mountains, that was setup in 1958.

2. Murugan Mala

Murugan Mala is a scenic hill in the region famous for the splendid rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Murugan. This idyllic location gives a soothing feel.

3. Mini Suicide Point

Mini suicide point in Vagamon is one of the most beautiful locations to visit. From this place, you can see the deep-seated valley below in its full beauty.

4. Tangal Para

Tangal Para is situated 2500 Meters above sea level. It has a spherical rock formation which is quite unique and has some spiritual significance among the people in the region.

5. Pine Forest

This place has nothing for you to see, but a lot to feel. Vagamon Pine Forest is a region with acres occupied by magnificent Pine trees thriving in the environment on steep slopes which is very unique to the region.

6. Orchid Garden

V​agamon Orchidarium has more than 3000 varieties of both indoor and outdoor orchids, 200 varieties of roses, and various other plants.

7. Paragliding

Behold the heavenly ambiance of Vagamon from a cloud-piercing height of 3000 feet with the paragliding activity. Enjoy the beautiful scenery of the western ghats of Kerala from high above.

8. Vagamon Suicide Point

Vagamon's Suicide Point, also called Moonpara is an awe-inspiring place to visit. It is a gorge that is V-shaped and one of the most beautiful locations in Vagamon.

9. Vagamon Meadows

The green grassland of Vagamon, surrounded by the hills of Vagamon, greenery, cool breeze, and mist will take your worries and stress.

10. Lower Pine Valley

It's a beautiful place with a thick pine forest. Very good to spend time with family and friends. This place is very peaceful and calm too.

11. Vagamon Tea Lake

The Vagamon Lake is a picturesque place amidst three green hills and lush green tea estates in the valley of Vagamon. Boasting picturesque surroundings, it is a famous tourist place that draws a lot of tourists around the year.

12. Vagamon Town

Vagamon, Kerala is a small hill station that emerges from within the borders of the Idukki District. This sleepy town, which was once a quiet and forgotten land is now filled with life and beautiful people.

13. Kottamala Point

“The forest is not a resource for us, it is life itself. It is the only place for us to live.” - Evaristo Nugkuag

14. Idukki Dam Catchment Area

Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Idukki dam catchment area in Vagamon. This idyllic place magically blends the beauty of the calm water body and the lush greenery.

15. Forest Road View

“And into the forest I go to lose my mind and find my soul.”- - John Muir

16. Waterfalls of Vagamon

Situated amidst dense vegetation, Maramala Waterfalls is a breathtaking natural water body that cascades from a height of 60 meters.

17. Uluppuni Tunnels

Uluppuni tunnel is a tunnel of about 3 km. The excess water from Kulamavu Dam is diverted towards this tunnel. It is recommended to avoid visiting Uluppuni during heavy rains and hot sunny days. Try to choose a pleasant dry climate while visiting.

18. Film Location

Famous shooting location in Vagamon. Lots of movies are shot here from various periods from multiple languages.

19. Hill View Point

Hill view point in Vagamon is a very exciting place to visit. The magnificent sight and the cool and fresh air will remain as a delightful memory from Vagamon.
