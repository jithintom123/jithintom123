Attractions

Want to explore the scenic beauty of Vagamon? Here are the list of places which will take you to the mesmerizing beauty of nature.
You can take the aid of your resort guide so that you don't miss a chance to explore these breathtaking views of Vagamon.

1. Kurisumala

Kurisumala Ashram is a Trappist Monastery in Syro-Malankara Catholic Church in the Sahya Mountains, that was setup in 1958.

2. MuruganMala

Murugan Mala is a scenic hill in the region famous for splendid rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Murugan. the atmosphere is often divine and attraction helps one get closer to nature.

3. Mini Suicide Point

Mini suicide point in vagamon is one of the beautiful locations in Vagamon. From this place, you can see the deep-seated valley below.

4. Tangal Para

Tangal Para situated 2500 Meter above the sea Level. it has a spherical rock formation which is quite unique and has some spiritual significance in the region.

5. Pine Forest

This place has nothing for you to see but a lot to feel.Vagamon Pine Forest is a region with acres occupied by magnificent Pine trees thriving in the environment on steep slopes which is very unique to the region

6. Orchid Garden

V​agamon Orchidarium has more than 3000 varieties of both indoor and outdoor orchids, 200 varieties of roses and various other plants.

7. Paragliding

Behold the heavenly ambiance of Vagamon from a cloud-piercing height of 3000 feet with this paragliding activity. Enjoy the beautiful scenery of the western ghats of Kerala from high above.

8. Vagamon Suicide Point

Vagamon also prides in having a Suicide Point; it is called Moonpara. It is a gorge which is V-shaped. It is one of the beautiful locations in Vagamon. From this place, you can see the deep-seated valley below.

9. Vagamon Meadows

The green grassland of Vagamon, surrounded by the hills of Vagamon, greenery, cool breeze and mist will take your worries and stress

10. Lower Pine Valley

It's a beautiful place with thick pine forest. Very good to spend time with family and friends.very peaceful and calm.

11. Vagamon Tea Lake

The Vagamon Lake is a picturesque place amidst three green hills and lush green tea estates in the valley of Vagamon. Boasting picturesque surroundings, it is a famous tourist place that draws a lot of tourists as part of Vagamon holiday packages.

12. Vagamon Town

Vagamon in Kerala is a small hill station that emerges from within the borders of Idukki District. This sleepy town, which was once a quiet and forgotten land is now the eye of major tourist activity.

13. Kottamala Point

“The forest is not a resource for us, it is life itself. It is the only place for us to live.” - Evaristo Nugkuag

14. Idukki Dam Catchment Area

The Idukki Dam across the beautiful Periyar River is a geometric marvel. The construct of the dam which is a double curvature arch gravitates people all over the country and enlists itself as one of the Vagamon Kerala tourist places.

15. Forest Road View

“And into the forest I go to lose my mind and find my soul.”- - John Muir

16. WaterFalls

Situated amidst dense vegetation, Maramala Waterfalls is a breathtaking natural water body that cascades from a height of 60 metres.

17. Uluppuni Tunnels

Uluppuni tunnel is a tunnel of about 3 km. The excess water from Kulamavu Dam is diverted towards this tunnel. It is recommended to avoid visiting Uluppuni during heavy rains and hot sunny days. Try to choose a pleasant dry climate.

18. Film Location

Famous shooting location in kerala. Lot of movies are shooted here.

19. Hill View Point

Vagamon is a serene and picturesque town that is placed on the border of Idukki-Kottayam districts on Idukki’s side. It is one of the best hill stations of Kerala.
