Delicacies

Pazhampori with Tea

It is one of the snacks you're likely to find at most tea shops or tiny eateries. The banana is the 'hero ingredient' in this dish. This tasty dish is prepared using nendran banana coated with maida flour.A pinch of turmeric gives its an yellow colour. Have it when u have a cup of tea while enjoying the beauty of Vagamon!!!

Parippuvada with Black Tea

Parippu vada is the perfect snack for a Malayalee.It is spicy,crunchy and above all yummy. Have it with a cup of black tea to enjoy it to the fullest!!!

Appam with Egg Roast

Appam with egg curry,commonly known as ""naadan mutta roast"" is a breakfast special dish of Kerala. The soft and fluffy Appam is made with fermented rice and coconut batter. The aromatic egg curry along with this appam makes it a good combo!!!

Puttu and Kadala

Puttu Kadala curry is treasured breakfast dish of God's own country. Puttu is a cylindrical steamed rice cake that is cooked in a mould with grated coconut. Kadala curry((black chickpeas curry) ) is the evergreen combination for puttu. The combination is awesome and you should taste it to know what it mean.

Porotta and Beef

Soft and flaky porotta with crispy edges, soaked in spicy and flavoursome beef gravy is the dream of any porotta lover.

Kappa Biriyani

This Thattukada Special recipe can be served as a snack or as a main course dish. Kappa Biriyani can be prepared with Mutton and chicken as well but the combination with beef is the best. It is a mix of Kappa (tapioca) and beef (with bones).

Naadan oonu

A normal Kerala Lunch also know as 'naadan oonu' consists of Plain Boiled Rice served with Vegetarian gravies and side dishes. Without a fish fry kerala oonu is incomplete!!!
